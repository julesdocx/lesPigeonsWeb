22 to 24 October 2021, the 25 young visual artists among us are organising their very first exhibition at the Novanois in Schaarbeek, BXL.

We are delighted to be able to organise this "tour d'artistes" in the beautiful Novanois, with the cherry on the cake that we have also been able to integrate young musicians and bands into this exhibition.

With this philosophy, we offer returning visitors a unique opportunity to follow the personal and artistic growth of each young artist at close quarters.

And who knows, maybe this day will also bring you to a moment of self-reflection:

Why am I, "the pigeon"?


Projet Pigeons x Novanoïs

As pigeons, we were in dire need of a nest, a place where ideas could hatch and take form.

During our search for a permanent home, Muriël Gyselbrecht, director of the Novanoïs, offered us a temporary shelter.

The pigeons offered to help her wherever help was needed and so it happened that naturally old traditions entered the modern way of life again: collaborations based on exchange and trust.

The NovanoÏs is a hidden gem in Schaerbeek, a very charming location that rents out its music studios and dance hall.

But where the pigeons prefer to brood, is high up in the old cinema hall...
